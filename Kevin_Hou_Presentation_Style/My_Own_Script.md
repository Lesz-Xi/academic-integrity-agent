1. Project Title & Description
The problem that I am trying to solve is the Al Detection Crisis. Most students or even professionals are constantly anxious about being falsely accused of using Al by black-box detectors that are often inaccurate also known as false-positives. 


Sovereignty Shield web app solves this by acting as a '**Flight Recorder**' for writing essays. I called it 'Flight Recored' because it records every keystroke and edit you make in Draft Mode. 

Keystroke Telemetry: I used **keystroke Telemetry** features that can capture unique typing patterns. This includes every objects that containts character and timestamp.

The second feature, "Cryptographic Hash Chains" this is a  way to ensure that the data is not tampered with. If the log is modified, the hash breaks.

I also included **Real-time Snapshots**, which provide automatic draft versioning to show the evolution of your work over time.

And finally, **PDF Attestation**. This generates verifiable certificates of authorship that serve as final proof that you wrote the content.

I think having all these features together, you can prove your authorship without being falsely accused of using Al.
